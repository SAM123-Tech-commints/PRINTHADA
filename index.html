About

Rooted in 15 years of industry experience, Printhada specializes in high-quality advertising fabrication and printing at reasonable costs. We provide everything from corporate apparel and souvenirs to secure, QR-coded document printing designed to safeguard authenticity. By prioritizing realistic lead times and direct communication via Gmail and Messenger, we ensure a seamless and professional experience for every client.
